Frequently Asked Questions

What are the team's practice hours?

Our team practices every weekday from 4:00 PM to 6:00 PM at the local cricket ground. Additional practice sessions may be scheduled during weekends depending on match schedules.

How can I join the team?

To join Thunder Bolt XI, you can fill out the membership form available on our website or contact our team manager directly. Please note that membership openings may vary depending on team requirements.

Where can I find information about upcoming matches?

You can find information about upcoming matches on our Matches page. We regularly update the schedule with match dates, timings, and venues.

How and where can I play my first match?

You can play your first match by becoming a member of our team first! Then in out home league(TBL), you can have your first match.
